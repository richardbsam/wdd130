Port Harcourt

Port Harcourt is a vibrant and populous city located in the southern part of Nigeria, in the Niger Delta region. It serves as the capital of Rivers State and is renowned for its strategic location along the Bonny River, making it a significant hub for trade and commerce. The city is a major center for Nigeria's oil and gas industry, with numerous multinational companies operating in the area. Despite its economic importance, Port Harcourt has faced challenges related to urban development and infrastructure, but it continues to be a dynamic and culturally diverse metropolis with a rich history.
